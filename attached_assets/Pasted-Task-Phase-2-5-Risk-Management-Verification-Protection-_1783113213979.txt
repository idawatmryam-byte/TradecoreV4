Task: Phase 2.5 – Risk Management Verification & Protection

This is NOT Phase 3. Do NOT modify my trading strategy, indicators, entry logic, AI logic, coin selection, or optimization settings. Your only task is to audit, verify, and strengthen the existing risk management system to ensure it is mathematically correct, reliable, and safe.

Requirements:

1. Position Sizing
- Verify that every trade risks exactly the configured percentage of the account (currently 1%).
- Position size must always be calculated using:
  - Current account balance
  - Entry price
  - Stop-loss distance
- If the stop-loss becomes wider, position size must automatically decrease.
- If the stop-loss becomes tighter, position size must automatically increase.
- Verify there are no bugs that cause inconsistent position sizing.

2. Stop Loss
- Ensure every trade immediately receives a valid Stop Loss after execution.
- Verify the Stop Loss price is calculated correctly using the configured ATR multiplier.
- Ensure the Stop Loss cannot accidentally be skipped or ignored.
- Ensure the bot never intentionally loses more than the configured risk because of calculation errors.

3. Take Profit
- Ensure every trade immediately receives a valid Take Profit after execution.
- Verify the Take Profit price is calculated correctly.
- Verify TP and SL remain active until the trade closes unless intentionally modified by the strategy.

4. Risk Validation
For every completed trade calculate and log:
- Entry Price
- Exit Price
- Stop Loss Price
- Take Profit Price
- ATR Value
- Position Size
- Expected Maximum Loss
- Actual Loss
- Expected Profit
- Actual Profit
- Trading Fees
- Slippage (if measurable)
- Exit Reason (Stop Loss, Take Profit, Timeout, Opposite Signal, etc.)

5. Risk Protection
After every trade compare:
- Expected Maximum Loss
- Actual Loss

If Actual Loss exceeds Expected Loss by more than a small tolerance (fees + normal slippage), log a WARNING with the exact reason.

If repeated risk violations are detected:
- Pause opening new trades.
- Log the issue.
- Send a Telegram/Discord alert:
"Risk management violation detected. Trading paused."

6. Code Audit
Review the entire trading engine for:
- Incorrect position sizing calculations.
- Incorrect Stop Loss calculations.
- Incorrect Take Profit calculations.
- Missing protective orders.
- Any bug that could cause a trade to lose more than the configured risk.
- Any logic that allows trades to remain open without proper protection.

7. Final Report
After the audit, provide a detailed report containing:
- Whether the position sizing is mathematically correct.
- Whether Stop Loss calculations are correct.
- Whether Take Profit calculations are correct.
- Whether every trade is protected by valid SL and TP orders.
- Whether any bugs were found and fixed.
- Whether the bot can ever lose more than the configured risk under normal market conditions.
- A summary of every change made.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT change my strategy.
Do NOT change my indicators.
Do NOT change my entry logic.
Do NOT change my ATR multipliers.
Do NOT change my Take Profit or Stop Loss settings.
Do NOT optimize the strategy.

Your only objective is to make the current risk management system accurate, reliable, fully validated, and safe while preserving all existing trading behavior.
