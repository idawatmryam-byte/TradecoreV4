TradeCore Phase 2 – Multi-Strategy Expansion

IMPORTANT: Build on top of the completed Phase 1 engine. Do NOT create a separate engine. Reuse the existing scanner, indicator engine, market regime engine, risk engine, and backtesting system. Keep the current API, database, dashboard, paper trading, and live trading fully compatible. Do NOT modify my .env, Binance API keys, database credentials, VPS configuration, or PM2 setup.

OBJECTIVE
Transform TradeCore from a single-strategy bot into a multi-strategy quantitative trading platform. The engine should evaluate several specialized strategies simultaneously and execute only the highest-quality opportunities.

==================================================
1. STRATEGY FRAMEWORK
==================================================

Create a modular strategy framework where each strategy implements a common interface.

Each strategy must provide:
- strategyId
- strategyName
- supportedRegimes
- evaluate(symbol, mtf, indicators, regime)
- confidenceScore
- entryReason
- suggestedSL
- suggestedTP
- suggestedHoldingTime

The main engine should collect signals from all strategies, rank them by confidence, pass them through the risk engine, and execute only the best opportunities.

==================================================
2. MOMENTUM BREAKOUT STRATEGY
==================================================

Entry when:
- Price breaks recent high
- Volume is above average
- ADX confirms trend strength
- 1h trend is aligned

Use ATR-based stop and dynamic take profit.

==================================================
3. TREND PULLBACK STRATEGY
==================================================

Entry when:
- Strong higher-timeframe trend
- Price pulls back to EMA20 or EMA50
- Bullish candle confirmation
- Momentum resumes in trend direction

This should become the primary trend-following strategy.

==================================================
4. MEAN REVERSION STRATEGY
==================================================

Only active during RANGE regime.

Entry when:
- Price reaches Bollinger Band extremes
- RSI is overextended
- ADX indicates weak trend
- Price begins reverting toward VWAP or the middle band

==================================================
5. VWAP REVERSION STRATEGY
==================================================

Entry when:
- Price stretches significantly away from VWAP
- Momentum slows
- Volume decreases
- Reversion probability increases

==================================================
6. MICRO SCALPING STRATEGY
==================================================

Designed for 1m and 3m charts.

Entry when:
- Short-term momentum spike
- Volume surge
- Tight spread
- Trend alignment with 5m and 15m

Target quick ATR-based exits and short holding times.

==================================================
7. VOLATILITY BREAKOUT STRATEGY
==================================================

Entry when:
- Bollinger Band squeeze
- ATR expansion begins
- Volume increases
- Price breaks consolidation range

==================================================
8. STRATEGY SELECTION ENGINE
==================================================

For every symbol:

1. Detect market regime
2. Run all strategies that support that regime
3. Calculate confidence for each strategy
4. Rank all signals across all symbols
5. Execute only the highest-ranked opportunities

Example:

SOL – Momentum Breakout – 92
BTC – Pullback – 88
ETH – Mean Reversion – 81
XRP – Scalping – 95

Bot chooses XRP first, then SOL, then BTC if risk limits allow.

==================================================
9. STRATEGY-SPECIFIC RISK SETTINGS
==================================================

Allow each strategy to have its own:
- Risk %
- ATR multiplier
- TP multiplier
- Trailing stop settings
- Maximum holding time
- Maximum concurrent positions
- Minimum confidence threshold

==================================================
10. PORTFOLIO EXPOSURE IMPROVEMENTS
==================================================

Add:
- Correlation scoring
- Sector exposure limits
- Maximum exposure per strategy
- Maximum exposure per symbol
- Long/short exposure tracking

==================================================
11. DATABASE UPGRADES
==================================================

Add fields/tables for:
- strategy_id
- strategy_name
- strategy_confidence
- market_regime
- signal_rank
- portfolio_exposure
- strategy_performance

Do NOT remove existing tables.

==================================================

- Strategy comparison
