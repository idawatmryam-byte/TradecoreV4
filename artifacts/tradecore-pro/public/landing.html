Why it's different

Most trading bots are a black box. This one shows its work.

Your keys, your custody

Connect your own Binance API keys and OANDA token, encrypted at rest. Funds never leave your own broker accounts and are never pooled with anyone else's.

Transparent by default

Every trade is logged, charted against its own stop and target, and explained after it closes. Nothing happens you can't see and audit.

Honest backtesting

The backtester runs the live engine's exact logic — same signals, sizing, and fees — so a result on history means what it says.

What's inside

A complete trading platform, not a script.

MarketsCrypto & Forex, side by side

Two independent trading sections that run simultaneously: Binance spot & USDⓈ-M futures (long/short, leverage, isolated or cross margin) and OANDA forex — currency majors, gold, and US index CFDs with atomic stop-protected entries and real market-hours awareness.

EngineMulti-strategy core

Eight configurable strategies with market-regime detection route across the market in parallel — each working its own budget, none crowding out the others.

RiskDollar-based sizing

State a fixed dollar risk and target; the engine derives position size, stop price, take-profit, reward:risk, and liquidation distance automatically.

SafetyReal protection

Exchange-side stop-losses, liquidation guards, a portfolio-risk ceiling, a daily circuit breaker, and startup reconciliation of every open position.

CockpitLive dashboard

Real-time positions with live P&L, candlestick charts overlaid with your stop and target, and one-click manual close.

AccountsMulti-user

Registration, sessions, Google & Apple sign-in, and a full account manager — every user fully isolated from the next.

How it works

Live in three steps.

Step 1

Connect your keys

Add your own broker credentials — Binance keys for crypto, an OANDA token for forex. Paper trading on both (testnet / practice account), live when you're ready. Encrypted, and only ever used for your account.

Step 2

Set your strategies

Enable the strategies you want and give each one a plain-dollar risk and target. The engine handles the sizing and the stops.

Step 3

Watch it trade

It scans, enters, manages, and exits on its own — while you see every decision live and close anything by hand whenever you like.
